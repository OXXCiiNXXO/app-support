FAQ

How do I add a timer?

Tap the + button in the top-right corner. Choose from preset timers or create a custom one.

Can I run multiple timers at once?

Yes. MeatTimer supports running unlimited timers simultaneously.

Does MeatTimer collect my data?

No. MeatTimer stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

How do I change the language?

MeatTimer follows your device's system language. Change it in Settings → General → Language & Region.
