Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

MeatTimer ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our app.

Data Collection

MeatTimer does not collect any personal data.

All timer data — including preset selections, custom timers, and completion logs — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SwiftData). No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute any user data. Since no data leaves your device, there is nothing to share.

Permissions

MeatTimer requests the following permissions:

  • Audio — To play alarm sounds when a timer completes.

No network, camera, microphone, location, or contact permissions are requested.

Third-Party Services

MeatTimer does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising frameworks, or crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

MeatTimer is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children, as we collect no data at all.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app's privacy policy section and on this page.
