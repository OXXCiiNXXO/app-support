Pet Wallet

Privacy Policy

Your pet and expense data stay on your device. No account, ads, analytics, tracking, or cloud sync.

Overview

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Pet Wallet, bundle identifier com.ciin.PetWallet, is an offline, local-first utility for tracking pet care expenses.

Data Collection

Pet Wallet does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use accounts, analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, cloud sync, or third-party APIs.

Data On Device

Pet records and expense entries that you create are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. These records may include pet name, species, breed, birth date, notes, expense amount, date, category, and note.

If you delete the app, local app data may be removed from your device.

CSV Export

The app can export expense records to a CSV file. Export only happens when you choose to use the export feature. After export, any sharing or storage destination is controlled by the iOS share sheet and the apps or services you select.

Children

The app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children.
