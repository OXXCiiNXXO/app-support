FAQ

How do I add a timer?

Tap the + button in the top-right corner. Choose from preset timers or create a custom one with your own duration and label.

Can I run multiple timers at once?

Yes. MeatTimer supports running unlimited timers simultaneously. Each timer has its own countdown and alarm sound.

Does MeatTimer collect my data?

No. MeatTimer stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

How do I change the language?

MeatTimer follows your device's system language. Change it in Settings → General → Language & Region.

How do I stop an alarm?

When a timer completes, the alarm plays and the timer card highlights. Tap the timer card to dismiss the alarm.
