Privacy Policy

Classroom Math Facts

All data stays on your device.

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

Classroom Math Facts is a local-first app. All data you enter stays on your device. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, does not use analytics, does not track users, and makes no network requests.

Data You Enter

You may choose to enter practice settings such as grade level, operations, number range, and difficulty. Practice session history includes the problems presented, your answers, accuracy, and time spent. This information is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.

Data Collection

Classroom Math Facts does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking services.

Network Access

The app does not make any network requests. All operations — problem generation, PDF export, session history — work entirely offline.

Backups

If iCloud Backup is enabled on your device, local app data may be included in your device backup according to Apple's iCloud backup behavior. Classroom Math Facts does not control iCloud Backup.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email zpc6589@gmail.com.
