FAQ

Where is my data stored?

All pet records and expense data are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. The app does not require an account and does not upload your data to any server.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export expense data as a CSV file using the system share sheet. After export, the destination is controlled by the iOS share sheet and the app or service you select.

Will deleting the app delete my data?

Yes. Because all records are stored locally in the app, deleting the app can remove the local data stored by the app. Please export your data via CSV before deleting.

Does the app support multiple pets?

Yes. You can add multiple pets, each with its own expenses, categories, and spending summaries.
