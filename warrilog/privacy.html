Warrilog

Privacy Policy

Your warranty records stay on your device. No account, ads, analytics, tracking, or cloud sync.

Overview

Effective date: June 14, 2026

Warrilog, bundle identifier com.zhoupeichen.LocalWarrantyTracker, is an offline, local-first utility for recording product warranty information.

Data Collection

Warrilog does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use accounts, analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, cloud sync, or third-party APIs.

Data On Device

Warranty records that you create are stored locally on your device. These records may include item name, brand, purchase date, warranty period, price, notes, creation date, and update date.

If you delete the app, local app data may be removed from your device.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app schedules local reminders for warranty expiration dates. Notification scheduling happens on your device and does not send data to any server.

CSV Export

The app can export warranty records to a CSV file. Export only happens when you choose to use the export feature. After export, any sharing or storage destination is controlled by the iOS share sheet and the apps or services you select.
