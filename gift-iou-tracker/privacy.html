Privacy Policy

Gift & IOU Tracker

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Overview

Gift & IOU Tracker is a local-first app. All data you enter stays on your device. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, does not use analytics, and does not track users.

Data You Enter

You may choose to enter contact names, notes, gift records, IOUs, amounts, dates, and repayment reminder dates. This information is stored locally on your device.

Data Collection

Gift & IOU Tracker does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Notifications

If you set an expected repayment date, the app may ask for notification permission and schedule a local notification on your device. These notifications are handled by iOS.

Backups

If iCloud Backup is enabled on your device, local app data may be included in your device backup according to Apple's iCloud backup behavior. Gift & IOU Tracker does not control iCloud Backup.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email zpc6589@gmail.com.
