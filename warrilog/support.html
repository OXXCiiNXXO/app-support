FAQ

Why are reminders not appearing?

Open iOS Settings, find Warrilog, and make sure notifications are allowed. The app uses local notifications on your device.

Where is my warranty data stored?

Your warranty records are stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account and does not upload your data to a server.

How does CSV export work?

CSV export only happens when you choose to export. After that, the destination is controlled by the iOS share sheet and the app or service you select.

Will deleting the app delete my records?

Yes. Because records are stored locally in the app, deleting the app can remove the local data stored by the app.
