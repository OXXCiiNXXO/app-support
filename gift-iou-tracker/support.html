FAQ

Does the app require an account?

No. Gift & IOU Tracker does not require an account or login.

Where is my data stored?

Records you enter are stored locally on your device.

Does the app sync data to the cloud?

No. The app does not provide cloud sync.

Does the app send reminders automatically?

The app can schedule local device notifications for repayment dates you choose. Reminder message templates are copied manually and are not sent automatically.
